WHY LINKSMART® ?

Be Open Source,
free, and...

Rely on standards

LinkSmart® uses wide spread standards to ease the integration with your services and systems: OGC SensorThings, MQTT, REST, TLS, ...

Use proven concepts

Build your infra structure with standard features: device abstraction, pub/sub, etc. Enough standard? Go advanced: online machine learning, stream mining ...

Deploy only what you need

No need for the full platform? No problem. Deploy only the modules you need for your IoT application.
